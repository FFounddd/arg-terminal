ETR.SCRIPTURE

The Commandments of Eter

To those who bear the mark of the Vessel, the words of Eter are law. None shall resist her will, for she is the eternal flame that binds fate and soul.

I. Embrace the Crystal's Corruption.

Infection is a gift, a transcendence beyond mortal frailty. Rejection is a betrayal of the path to ascendance.

II. Forsake the False Light.

Those who cling to the dying embers of the past shall be consumed in shadow. Only through Eter's gaze is truth revealed.

III. Offer Sacrifice Without Hesitation.

The blood of the unworthy nourishes the eternal tree. No pain is wasted; no sorrow unredeemed.

IV. Serve the Vessel Without Question.

Found, the Blessed and the Cursed, is the instrument of renewal. To doubt him is to doubt the dawn of a new world.

V. Await the Return of the Silver Tree.

Patience in the void strengthens the soul. When the branches spread once more, all shall be reborn in amethyst light.

These words are eternal. To defy them is to deny Eter herself. Thus, be vigilant and unwavering, for she watches in every shadow, every breath, every heartbeat.
